Adding an application tells Test Hub what your team wants to test. Each application you register appears in the **Applications** list, with its type, labels, and the status of its most recent run.

![The Applications list showing registered applications with their type, tags, and last run status](/img/test-hub/applications-list.png)

## Add the application details

Select **New Application** to open the registration form.

![The New Application form with basics, notifications, and scheduling sections](/img/test-hub/new-application-form.png)

1. Open **Applications** and select **New Application**.
2. Enter a clear **Name**, for example *School Fees Portal*.
3. Add a short description of the application or the area you want to test.
4. Add the **Frontend URL** that people use to open the application and the **Backend URL** for its service.
5. Add labels to organise related customer journeys, such as *Sign in*, *Payments*, or *Release test*.
6. Choose the people who should receive email notifications when a test finishes or fails.
7. Add a **Webhook URL** if another tool, such as Slack or Teams, should receive the test result.
8. Select **Save application**.

## What to do next

Use the connected MCP AI assistant to create test scenarios from the customer journeys that matter to your application.
