Test Hub helps teams answer a simple question: **is our application ready?**

It tests the customer journeys that matter to your application, such as signing in, making a payment, submitting an application, or completing an order. Use the connected MCP AI assistant to create test scenarios in plain language, then run those tests before a release, automatically on a schedule, or whenever your team needs an answer.

## Why teams use Test Hub

- **Save time before releases** by repeating important tests in minutes rather than testing each journey by hand.
- **Create test scenarios in plain language** by describing the customer journey to the connected MCP AI assistant.
- **Spot issues early** when a key customer journey stops working.
- **Focus discussions** with clear pass/fail results and a replay of what went wrong.
- **Keep everyone informed** by sending results to the people responsible for the application.

<Callout type="info" title="Getting Test Hub into your application">
  Test Hub is available through the [Product modules](/docs/product-modules). A SolidX administrator can install it for your application; this guide then shows your team how to use it.
</Callout>
