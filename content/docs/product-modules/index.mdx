## Overview

Product modules are ready-to-use SolidX modules you can discover and add to your application. Each module adds a complete business capability to your existing SolidX application, so your team can start using it without building that capability from the beginning.

A module is delivered as a SolidX package, with the `.sldx` file extension. Your SolidX administrator imports this package into your application, completes the guided setup, and makes the new workspace available to the right people.
