Testing Hub works best when the people who can make a decision see the result quickly. Add the product owner, delivery lead, and the team responsible for fixing issues to each product.

## Add your team

Open the product and add the people who should receive its updates. They can then be included in alerts for successful tests, failed tests, or both.

## A simple notification approach

- Tell the core product and delivery team when a test **needs attention**.
- Tell a wider group about successful tests only when that helps a release or business process.
- Review the list when team ownership changes.

<Callout type="info" title="Make alerts useful">
  Send alerts to the people who can act on them. Too many recipients can make important results easier to miss.
</Callout>
