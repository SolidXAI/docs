Adding a product tells Testing Hub what your team wants to monitor. Use a name that everyone recognises.

## Add the essentials

1. Open **Applications** and select **New Application**.
2. Enter a clear **Name**, for example *School Fees Portal*.
3. Add a short description explaining the product or the area you want to protect.
4. Add labels that help your team organise customer journeys.
5. Choose the people who should receive a notification when a test finishes or needs attention.
6. Add a **Webhook URL** if another tool, such as Slack or Teams, should receive the test result.
7. Select **Save application**.

## Keep people and tools informed

Use **Notifications** to tell the people responsible for the product when a test finishes or needs attention. Use a **Webhook** when the same result should also be sent to another tool your team uses, such as Slack or Teams.

See [Choose Notifications](/docs/module-marketplace/testing-hub/automation/notifications) and [Send Results to Other Tools](/docs/module-marketplace/testing-hub/automation/webhooks).

## What to do next

After adding the product, agree on the customer journeys that matter most. Then use the connected MCP AI assistant to create test scenarios from those journeys. Start with a short list of high-impact journeys, then use [labels](/docs/module-marketplace/testing-hub/applications/tags) to keep them organised.
