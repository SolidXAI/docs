Each product in Testing Hub represents something your team wants to keep working well: a customer portal, an internal service, a website, or a key part of a larger product.

The **Applications** screen is where you see all of these products, open their recent results, and start tests when you need them.

  </Card>
  <Card icon={<Tag />} title="Group important tests" href="/docs/module-marketplace/testing-hub/applications/tags">
    Use labels such as Payments, Sign in, or Release test to focus on the right journeys.
  </Card>
  <Card icon={<Users />} title="Choose who stays informed" href="/docs/module-marketplace/testing-hub/applications/team-and-users">
