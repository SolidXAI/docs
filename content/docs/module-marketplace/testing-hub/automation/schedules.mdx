Scheduling removes the need for someone to remember routine product tests. Choose the customer journeys, how often they should run, and who should receive the result.

## Set up a schedule

1. Open the product you want to monitor.
2. Turn on **Run on a schedule**.
3. Choose a clear frequency, such as every weekday morning or once a day.
4. Select the labels that identify the journeys to include, if you do not want to run everything.
5. Choose who should be informed when the run finishes.
6. Save your changes.

## Review and pause a schedule

All scheduling details are available on the product's **Application** screen. Open the product to review its frequency, selected labels, and notification recipients. Turn **Run on a schedule** off to pause it, then turn it back on when you are ready.

<Callout type="tip" title="Choose a schedule people can act on">
  A morning test is useful when someone can review a problem before customers are affected. Choose a time that fits your team's working day.
</Callout>
