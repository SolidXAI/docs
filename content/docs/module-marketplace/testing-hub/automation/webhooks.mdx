---
title: Send Results to Other Tools
icon: "webhook"
description: Send test results to the tools your team already uses to stay informed.
---

Testing Hub can send the outcome of a test run to another tool through a **Webhook**. This is useful when your team already follows product and delivery updates in a shared place, such as Slack or Teams.

## Add a webhook

1. Open the product in **Applications**.
2. Add the Webhook URL provided by the tool your team uses.
3. Save the product.

When a test run finishes, that tool receives the result and can show it to the people who need to know.

<Callout type="tip" title="Use webhooks alongside notifications">
  Use notifications for people who need an email. Use a webhook when your team also needs the result in another shared tool.
</Callout>
