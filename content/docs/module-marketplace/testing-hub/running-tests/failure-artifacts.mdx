When a customer journey needs attention, Testing Hub gives your team a clear starting point for the conversation. Instead of trying to reproduce the issue from a short message, you can review what happened during the test.

## Watch the replay

For a failed journey in the browser, open the run and play the recorded replay. It shows the journey as it happened and makes it easier to explain the issue to the delivery team.

## Share a useful report

When you raise the issue, include:

- The name of the customer journey that needs attention.
- The time of the test run.
- The replay or link to the result.
- The product impact, for example: “Parents cannot complete a payment.”

Your delivery team can use the supporting information in Testing Hub to investigate further. As a product owner, you can stay focused on the customer impact and whether the journey is working again after the fix.
