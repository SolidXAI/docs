Scheduling removes the need for someone to remember routine application tests. All scheduling details are available on the application's **Application** screen.

## Schedule tests

1. Open the application in **Applications**.
2. Turn on **Run on a schedule**.
3. Choose how often the test scenarios should run.
4. Select labels if you want to run only a group of scenarios.
5. Choose who should receive the result.
6. Save your changes.

Turn **Run on a schedule** off to pause the schedule, then turn it back on when you are ready.

<Callout type="tip" title="Choose a schedule people can act on">
  A morning test is useful when someone can review a problem before customers are affected. Choose a time that fits your team's working day.
</Callout>
